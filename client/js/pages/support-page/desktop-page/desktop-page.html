RaterPRO Desktop

Any contact with a My ENERGY STAR Account (MESA) can be added to an organization’s RaterPRO account. You can add additional contacts through MESA or by contacting energystarhomes@energystar.gov.

When an organization begins using RaterPRO, an account invitation e-mail will be sent to the primary contact. That primary RaterPRO user can then invite additional users on the ‘Users’ tab.

The Users tab shows a list of eligible users, based on the organizations MESA account. Users with Rater, Rater Admin or Provider permissions checked already have accounts. To invite a new user, select one or more permissions check boxes (Rater, Rater Admin or Provider) for that user and click ‘Save’, which will trigger an automated invitation e-mail to the email address listed for that user.

Note: e-mail addresses are based on contact’s My ENERGY STAR Account (MESA) and, if incorrect, should be updated through MESA. Updates will be reflected in RaterPRO within 24 hours.

When a new home is ready to be rated it’s time to create a job within RaterPRO. On the Rater Jobs tab, click Create a Job.

  • Rating Type – Indicate if this is an ENERGY STAR rating, or a HERS-only rating
  • Sample Set – Indicate if this is a ‘confirmed’ rating or a sampling rating type. Sample sets can include up to seven homes.
  • Rating File – Jobs are pre-populated with the expected features from the projected energy model. Either select the XML file directly from your computer or from your Template Library. The Template Library is useful for files you will use repeatedly.
  • Builder and Location – Review the auto-populated builder and location information and update as necessary.
  • Export Filename – Now is a good time to enter a meaningful filename for the XML file that will be exported at the job’s completion. This might include the address or other identifying information to set it apart from other files in your downloads folder.
  • HVAC Design Report and Rater Design Review Checklist – Optionally attach PDF copies of these reports for review in the field.

When ready, click Create.

Once a job is complete, it is suggested to download and store an Archival Report in PDF format for your records. The Archival report includes job data like the status of each inspection item and the Job History. Note that the report does not include the minimum rated feature values themselves, for example the insulation R-values, because this information is already included in the XML file.

To download the report, open the job and select ‘Download Archival Report’ from the dropdown arrow menu.

Download Archival Report

Optionally, users may share completed job information with Providers, for use during their quality assurance review. Once shared, the job information will be permanently available to the provider and cannot be “unshared” by a rating company. This ensures the Provider has a permanent record of the information used during their QA review.

Share Single Job: Open the job and click the ‘Submit to Provider’ icon at the top of the screen for an individual job.

Submit Job to Provider

Share Multiple Jobs: On the Rater Jobs list, check the job(s) you’d like to share, and click ‘Submit to Provider’.

Submit Multiple Jobs

Once submitted, Providers may leave comments from their quality assurance review. To access these comments, open the job and click ‘Job Comments’ on the bottom bar.

Job Comments
