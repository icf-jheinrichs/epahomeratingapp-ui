RaterPRO Mobile App

The RaterPRO mobile app is used by Raters to perform inspections in the field. The typical workflow is to select the day’s jobs, make them Available Offline, and ensure the sync status is up to date before leaving for the day. Perform inspections and make edits as needed. At the end of the day, open up the RaterPRO app when back on a network and let the app complete a sync.

Here are the key features of the RaterPRO app’s landing page:

Tabs along the top allow filtering the jobs list by status (for example, completed jobs)

Sync Status is green when the app is on network and fully synced with the cloud. Blue indicates syncing is in progress. Grey indicates the app is offline. Yellow means there are active edits ready to sync as soon as the app gets back online.

Completion Percentages circles show the fraction of pre-drywall and final items that are completed.

Available Offline toggles allow syncing all assets, including photos and PDFs, for viewing offline.

Create a Job button lets you create new jobs on the fly from any rating file loaded into your Template Library.

RaterPRO Mobile App

Before heading out for an inspection, it’s best to make those jobs available offline. This will guarantee that all job information, including photos and attached PDFs, are viewable even without a network connection.

Toggle the ‘Available Offline’ button to green to make the job available in offline mode. Wait for the Sync Status to return to green to ensure all jobs have been completely loaded for offline access. The job(s) can now be easily accessed on the Offline Jobs tab.

Toggle Offline Availability Offline Jobs Tab

Textual information for all jobs, regardless of offline status, are available for users offline. The only information not accessible are pictures and PDFs.

Select a job from the list to open. Inspection items are organized on six tabs. Within each tab, items are further organized into pre-drywall and final inspection sections. A colored bar above each stab shows the pre-drywall completion percentage, and the bar below each tab show the final completion percentage.

Minimum Rated Features are shown for all jobs. Scroll left and right in each table to see full details. Click a row of a table to edit details.

ENERGY STAR Checklist Items are shown for ENERGY STAR jobs.

Status buttons (Rater Verified, N/A, etc.) are next to each inspection item

Comment buttons can capture text comments and photos.

Job Details

Scroll left-right through tables to view all table information.

By default, all inspection items are shown. Select the ‘Filter’ button to narrow down the list by stage (pre-drywall vs. final) or status (To-do or Must Correct). For example, on a pre-drywall inspection you can filter to only see the pre-drywall items.

Filter Inspection Items

Most fields can be edited in the RaterPRO Mobile App. To edit, find the row in the table that requires updating. Click on that row. Next, a details window titled ‘Edit Windows’ will appear.

Edit any field in the ‘Edit Windows’ screen, click on the cell to update the value and click ‘Save’ in the top right corner. The edited row will then be highlighted yellow to notate a change had been made in the field and confirmed as accurate data.

Even if you’re not editing, the ‘Edit Windows’ screen is a great tool to reference intricate job details.

Update the verification status for each checklist item as you go through a job. To change the verification status, select the updated status next to the checklist item.

Inspection Item Status Status Types

Comments are a useful way to capture observations in the field. To add comments to a checklist item, select the ‘Comment Bubble’ next to the item and select ‘New +’ in the comment window. Add a photo and/or any notes to the comment. Select ‘Done’ after entering all information.

To view the comment, select the ‘Comment Bubble’. All comments for an item will appear. You can add as many comments and/or photos to a checklist item as needed. To view an enlarged version of a photo in the app, simply click on it.

For quality assurance (QA) purposes, RaterPRO requires a front elevation photo for each job. The front elevation photo can be taken at any point during the construction process. To add the front elevation photo:

  • Expand the bottom bar by selecting the ‘More’ icon
  • A small window will appear that contains a short job summary
  • Click ‘Add Photo’
  • After a photo is uploaded, select the ‘Less’ icon to minimize the bottom bar again

Once a front elevation photo has been added, the ‘Complete’ button at the top of the screen will be colored white. This means the job can be designated as completed when all action items are finished.

Reminder: Take this photo while you are in the field. You will be unable to complete the job without this photo.
